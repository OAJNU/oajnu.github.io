Silba - Initiative for Dialogue and Democracy

A Danish youth organization that carries out election observation missions and international projects in tandem with organizations around the world. They partnered with OAJNU in order to create a project that would aim to empower the young people of Argentina.

Partnership

First Contact

(2020-2021)

Anne Sophie Noer, Silba’s president at the time, contacted us via email to express their interest in working alongside OAJNU in the framework of the DUF (Dansk Ungdoms Fællesråd - Danish Youth Council) funding for Danish organizations to create links with other social organizations around the world. She always expressed interest in traveling to Argentina to talk and get to know us better, but border restrictions due to the pandemic made it impossible to carry out the visit.

Visits to Denmark

(2022)

Valentina Testa and Paula Muscia, our President and Financing Secretary at the time, traveled to attend the Partner Seminar in Helsingor, Denmark, along with social leaders from other organizations. We were the only Latin American NGO present and participated in activities aimed at learning and strengthening the knowledge available about international partnerships and international project development.

Visits to Argentina

(2022)

Two members of Silba visited Argentina for their Preparatory Study. They visited the offices in Córdoba and Mendoza, where they met with our volunteer corps and learned about the work of our organization and the context of our country.

Team Meeting in Argentina

(2023)

To keep developing the Preparatory Study, one of Silba’s team members visited Salta, where she met up with Daiana Calisaya and Mauro Modica. They are part of the team involved in the pilot project and, together, had meetings where discussions took place and new ideas were proposed to ensure a good development of the project. During their time in this province, Silba’s member had the chance to meet with some volunteers from OAJNU’s offices there.

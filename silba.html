Partnership

First Contact

(2020-2021)

We were contacted via email by Anne Sophie Noer, their president at the time, expressing their interest in working together with OAJNU with the framework of DUF (Dansk Ungdoms Fællesråd - Danish Youth Council) funding for Danish organizations that create links with other social organizations around the world. She always expressed her intention to travel to Argentina to talk and get to know us better, but border restrictions due to the pandemic made it impossible to make the visit.

Visits to Denmark

(2022)

Our President back then, Valentina Testa, and the Financing Secretary, Paula Muscia, traveled to Helsingor, Denmark, to attend the Partner Seminar together with social leaders from other organizations. There, we were the only NGO from Latin America present, and participated in activities aimed at learning and strengthening knowledge about international partnerships and international project development.

Visits to Argentina

(2022)

two members of Silba visited Argentina for their Preparatory Study. They visited the headquarters in Cordoba and Mendoza, where they met our volunteer corps and learned about the work of our organization and the situation in our country.

Team Meeting in Argentina

(2023)

To keep going with the Preparatory Study, one of the Silba’s team member visited Salta where she met up with Daiana Calisaya and Mauro Modica who are part of the team involved in the pilot project, they had meetings with a lot of discussions and new ideas to ensure a good development of the pilot project, at the same time this Silba member had the chance to meet some volunteers of OAJNU’s headquarter in Salta.
